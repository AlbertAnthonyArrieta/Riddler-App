How to Play

This is a riddle game. Everytime you solve a riddle, you unlock the next level.

Type in your answer, and hit the submit button. You have unlimited attempts.

You answer should be a single letter word.

Correct: 'Dog'
Incorrect: 'A dog'

You can purchase hints for 200 coins and/or reveal the answer for 50 coins in the hints menu.

Each riddle you solve will grant you 25 coins.
